Feature(s)

Static Playlists

Due to a request I've added an option for Static Playlists. This works just like the static sidebar to force the playlists (right menu) to always be open rather than hidden under a menu icon.

Just like with static sidebar, I had to rearrange a bunch of items, so there's a good chance something might break/not look right. Feel free to let me know and I'll try and fix them up.
